Spiritual Formation Lite, v.2

September 7, 2025 Logan Augustine
📄 Click Here for PDF Preview

Every year, new students at Dallas Theological Seminary go through a year long course called Spiritual Formation. This course equips students to pursue Christlikeness through Spirit-led transformation, engaging with the key themes of identity, community, integrity, and fidelity within a relational, discipleship-focused context.

When I took this course in 2024 and joined a Spiritual Formations group, my faith grew in leaps and bounds. And when my good friend Virginia Giesler turned the SF content into SF Lite (v.1) for her discipleship groups, I was inspired to do the same for mine.

My SF Lite v.2 is a 14 week discipleship curriculum designed to help students develop a deeper understanding of themselves, their relationship with God, and their role in the body of Christ.
