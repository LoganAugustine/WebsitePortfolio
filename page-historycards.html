Church History Character Cards

February 23, 2026 Logan Augustine

In the Spring of 2026, I took my first course on Church History. I quickly discovered the course was really a study of individuals and their cotributions to history, and since I've always found success in making flashcards, I figured I'd need flashcards for this course as well. The challenge was that there are hundreds of people who are worth knowing from the history of our faith.

So, using my knowledge of coding in Python (and a lot of help from my friends ChatGPT and Claude AI), I created a database and script that generates these neat character cards. All of the information is based on the notes I've taken from Dr. John Hannah's HT5101 and Invitation to Church History.

You can find a full set of cards (that are constantly evolving as I take notes) on my GitHub page, along with the Python script and data files.
